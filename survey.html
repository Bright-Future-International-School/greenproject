For Greenery, We Will Always Stand.








Conducted Survey Among the Public & Students

This survey was conducted based on Sustainability & Greenery in Qatar. We've asked Age Groups between 17-20 and 30-45 about what they think about Qatar taking the steps to embrace sustainability and bring it within the country and community, in the peoples' daily lives and the environment which surround us. We've spoke with people around the public, teachers and even students, about their familiarity with Qatar's Vision 2023 for Sustainability and these were the results of our research.
